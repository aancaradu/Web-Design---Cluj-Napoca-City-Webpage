Cluj-Napoca stands out as a hub of academic excellence, home to prestigious universities and a vibrant scholarly community. Known for fostering innovation and intellectual growth, it attracts students and researchers from around the world. Here are presented the universities that make Cluj-Napoca a leader in education.

Based in the Romanian city of Cluj-Napoca, this technical university is home to nearly 20,000 students, making it the second-largest university in the city. The Technical University of Cluj-Napoca was founded in 1948 and initially had two departments: thermotechnics and machines. The university now has nine faculties in Cluj-Napoca, including the one I study at: Faculty of Electronics and Information Technology

The oldest medical education institution in Transylvania, the „Iuliu Hațieganu” University of Medicine and Pharmacy, has made history in the university education realm of Cluj. Today, the Iuliu Hațieganu University of Medicine and Pharmacy in Cluj-Napoca implements the principles outlined by Professor Hațieganu to fit into the context of international competitiveness, responding to the requirements of the European Space of Research and Knowledge.

Babeș-Bolyai University is Romania's largest higher education institution and a consistently strong performer in national and international rankings. Specialising in languages and law but offering courses across a large number of disciplines, UBB is in the city of Cluj-Napoca, often known as Cluj.

One of the most prestigious Life Sciences Universities in Romania, the University of Agricultural Sciences and Veterinary Medicine of Cluj-Napoca is constantly present in International Rankings. The university offers courses in all three cycles (Bachelor, Master, PhD) in the fields of agriculture, veterinary medicine, animal science and biotechnology, horticulture, food science and technology.

With a history of almost 100 years,The University of Art and Design in Cluj-Napoca is the most important artistic higher education institution in Transylvania whose fundamental mission is to provide an education of highest quality to those intending to follow a carrier in the field of the visual arts.

Gheorghe Dima National Music Academy is a public music-based institution that prides itself on modernizing the Romanian education system. The institution specializes in musical fields such as Opera Singing, Piano, Violin, harmony, and Counterpoint Composition. The university offers admission to both local and international students interested in pursuing their studies there.
