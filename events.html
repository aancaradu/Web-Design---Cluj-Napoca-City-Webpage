Untold Festival

Untold Festival is the largest electronic music festival held in Romania, taking place in Cluj-Napoca at the Cluj Arena. It is held annually and has been designated Best Major Festival in the European Festival Awards 2015.

Jazz in the Park

Jazz in the Park is a music festival in Romania, held annually in Cluj-Napoca. The festival covers an extensive scope of performances from contemporary jazz, funk, and blues to world music, alternative rock, acoustic projects, afrobeat, trip-hop, classical music, or a cappella.

Electric Castle

Electric Castle is a renowned music festival held annually at Banffy Castle near Cluj-Napoca, Romania. It blends live music, electronic beats, and stunning visuals in a unique historic setting. Known for its diverse lineup and vibrant atmosphere, it attracts music lovers from around the world.

Cluj Days

Colourful, creative, dynamic, active, and diverse, the celebration of our city reflects the community it is addressed to. Cluj Days is without any doubt the ideal context to create bonds between people. The festival closest to the hearts of the citizens engages the entire community in various activities and the city is full of emotion, smiles and a contagious optimism.

Transilvania International Film Festival

The Transilvania International Film Festival (TIFF) is the first international feature film festival in Romania, which is held annually in the historic capital of Transylvania, Cluj-Napoca.
